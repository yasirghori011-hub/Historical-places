Frequently Asked Questions

Explore interesting questions and facts about world history and heritage.

The New Seven Wonders of the World (2007) are:
1. Great Wall of China (China)
2. Petra (Jordan)
3. Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)
4. Machu Picchu (Peru)
5. Chichen Itza (Mexico)
6. Roman Colosseum (Italy)
7. Taj Mahal (India)
The Great Wall was built mainly during the reign of Emperor Qin Shi Huang (221–206 BCE) to protect China from northern invasions.
The Taj Mahal is admired for its white marble beauty and as a symbol of love. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
The pyramids were royal tombs for pharaohs. The Great Pyramid of Giza was built for Pharaoh Khufu around 2560 BCE.
The Roman Empire fell due to economic decline, invasions, political corruption, and instability, officially ending in 476 CE.
