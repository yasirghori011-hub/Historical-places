Great Wall of China

Great Wall of China duniya ki sabse lambi defensive wall hai, jo puranay China ke badshahon ne apne mulk ko dushmanon se bachanay ke liye banai thi. Ye wall Asia ke north side par banai gayi thi aur ye itni lambi hai ke space se bhi nazar aati hai.

  • Duniya ki sabse lambi defensive wall hai.
  • China ke north side par banai gayi thi dushmanon se bachne ke liye.
  • Construction shuru hui 7th century BC me.
  • Sabse zyada development hui Ming Dynasty (1368–1644 AD) ke dauran.
  • Total length takreeban 21,000 kilometers hai.
